Lost in Kiev’s progressive energy leads to oneiric epic post rock while still emitting the massive energy of post rock.

Their influences are Russian Circles, Red Sparowes, 65 Days of Statics, Cult of Luna, Maybeshewill, Mogwai… Lost in Kiev writes and records their own texts as an alternative to sampling movie soundbites which are commonly used in post rock tracks. Artwork and live video clips are also created by the band which generate an emotional and graphic cohesive experience… read more
